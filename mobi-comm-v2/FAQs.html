Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on the app?
Go to the Account page and click on 'Mobile'. Enter your Mobile Number and get OTP to create an account. Follow the simple on-screen instructions to complete your registration. Make sure to use a valid mobile number as you'll receive a verification code.
How can I recharge my mobile?
Go to the 'Recharge' section in the navigation menu, select your preferred plan or enter a custom amount. You can proceed with payment using various methods like cash, credit/debit cards, net banking, or digital wallets. Once the payment is successful, your recharge will be processed instantly.
What payment methods are available?
We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:
  • Cash payments at authorized retail outlets
  • Credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, etc.)
  • UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Net Banking for all major banks
  • Digital wallets like Paytm, Amazon Pay, etc.
All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.
How do I receive notifications about offers?
To stay updated with our latest offers and promotions:
  1. Go to the 'Settings' section in your profile
  2. Select 'Notifications Preferences'
  3. Choose your preferred notification channels: SMS, Email, or Push notifications
  4. You can also customize which types of offers you'd like to receive
We recommend enabling push notifications for instant updates about exclusive deals and time-sensitive offers.
How can I contact customer support?
We're here to help! You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels:
  • Through the 'Contact' section in the app
  • Send an email to 'support@mobi-comm.com'
  • Call our 24/7 helpline at +91 98765 43210
  • Visit any of our retail stores for in-person assistance
  • Chat with our virtual assistant within the app for instant responses
Our team is available 24/7 to address your queries and concerns.
What should I do if my recharge fails?
If your recharge fails, don't worry! First, check if the amount has been deducted from your account. If it has, the recharge might process automatically within 30 minutes. If not:
  1. Go to 'Transaction History' in your profile
  2. Find the failed transaction and click on 'Report Issue'
  3. Our system will verify and process the recharge or refund within 24-48 hours
For immediate assistance, you can contact our customer support.
How do I check my current plan details?
To check your current plan details:
  • Open the Mobi-comm app and go to the home screen
  • Your active plan will be displayed with remaining data, validity, and other benefits
  • For detailed information, tap on 'View Plan Details'
  • You can also check your plan by dialing *123# from your registered mobile number
The app also shows recommendations for your next recharge based on your usage patterns.
