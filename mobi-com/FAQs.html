FAQs - Mobi-comm

1. How do I register on the app?
Go to the Account page and click on 'Mobile'. Enter your Mobile Number and get OTP to create an account.
2. How can I recharge my mobile?
Go to the 'Recharge' section, choose a plan, and proceed with payment via cash or card.
3. What payment methods are available?
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and UPI payments.
4. How do I receive notifications about offers?
Enable notifications under 'Settings' to receive updates via SMS, email, or calls.
5. How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our support team through the 'Contact' section in the app or by sending an email to 'support@mobi-comm.com'.
